ATTRACTIONS

Duluth offers many exciting and interesting attractions for you to expierence during your time in Duluth

WINTER/SPRING

If you find yourself in Duluth during the winter, one place you must go is the tour of lights with a view of the water. This attraction started in someones backyard and grew so big it is now hosted at Bayfront in downtown Duluth. Take the kids and see over 100 different light displays, enjoy some free hot chocolate, and even meet santa clause.

Another must see during the winter time is a UMD hockey game. The University of Minnesota-Duluth is home to the back-to-back NCAA national champions. Check out the newly build Amsoil Arena, home of the Bulldogs, and enjoy some of the finest ameture hockey in the world.

SUMMER/FALL

During the summer one of the best times you can have is the weekend after the 4th of July at Bayfront. Home to the coverband concert with some great music, and even better scenery.

During the Fall one must take a drive up the scenic highway to Two Harbors, about an hour drive, to see some great scenery.
